$`M(5) = 100 \cdot b^5 = 0,7 \cdot 100`$

$`100 \cdot b^5 = 70`$

$`b^5 = 0{,}7`$

$`b = \sqrt[5]{0,7}`$

$`b \approx 0,931`$

$`M(24) = 100 \cdot b^{24} \approx 100\cdot0,931^{24}\approx 17,980`$
